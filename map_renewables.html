Renewable power plant map of Argentina

This map shows the location of the renewable power plants in Argentina. The data is provided by the argentinian government and it can be found here. It does not show power plants that are under the jurisdiction of two or more governments, such as Yacyretá. Projects under construction are shown as partial circles, for which progress level is shown by how much complete the circle is.

This map also doesn't include many renewable plants that do not appear in the data. I am working on adding data from other sources.

Technology and functionality

This project was created using Python 3.9, aided with the Folium library. Zoom in the markers with numbers to see the ungrouped power stations. Click or tap on the unnumbered markers to see their names. The code is available at my Github repo.
